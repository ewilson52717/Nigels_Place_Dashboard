Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 9, 2026

The short version: When you book pet care services or create a profile through this portal, we collect only the information needed to manage your pet's care. We never sell your personal information, and it is never shared with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

1. Who We Are

PawDesk is a pet care management platform developed by Spend by Design LLC ("PawDesk," "we," "us," or "our"). PawDesk provides the software that pet care businesses — such as doggy daycares, boarding facilities, and groomers — use to manage bookings, client profiles, and pet records.

When you use a booking portal or client account powered by PawDesk, both the pet care business and Spend by Design LLC may process personal information about you as described in this policy. Questions? Contact us at hello@spendbydesign.co.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or make a booking:

  • Your contact details – Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Emergency contact information – A secondary contact name and phone number for use during your pet's stay.
  • Your pet's profile – Pet name, breed, age, weight, and any behavioral notes or special care instructions you share.
  • Vaccination records – Documents or photos of your pet's vaccination history that you upload so the facility can confirm your pet is up to date.
  • Pet photos – A profile photo of your pet, used to help staff identify and care for your animal.
  • Booking details – Dates, services requested (e.g., full-day care, grooming), and any special instructions for a visit.
  • Photo consent preference – Whether you allow the facility to photograph your pet during visits for activity records.

We do not collect payment card numbers, bank account information, or government-issued ID numbers through this portal.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used to:

  • Create and manage your client account and your pet's profile.
  • Schedule, confirm, and track bookings for your pet's visits.
  • Verify that your pet's vaccinations meet the facility's requirements before a visit.
  • Communicate with you about upcoming bookings, reminders, and any changes.
  • Display activity photos taken during your pet's visits (if you have given consent).
  • Generate invoices and track payments for services rendered.
  • Respond to questions or concerns you raise about a booking or your pet's care.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes — ever.

Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • The pet care facility – The staff of the business you are booking with will have access to your profile, your pet's records, and your booking history so they can properly care for your animal.
  • Google LLC – PawDesk uses Google's services to securely store records on behalf of the pet care business. Google's role is limited to storing data; your information is not used for Google advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements – We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of people or animals in our care.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely on behalf of the pet care business using industry-standard Google cloud infrastructure. Access is protected by Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication and is restricted to authorized staff of the pet care facility and PawDesk administrators.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Google account.

6. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your personal information and your pet's records for as long as you maintain an active client relationship with the pet care facility. If you would like your information removed, please contact the pet care business directly or reach out to us at hello@spendbydesign.co, and we will assist in processing your request.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you and your pet.
  • Correct – Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile at any time through the client portal.
  • Delete – Request that we delete your account and personal information.
  • Withdraw consent – For example, you may withdraw photo consent at any time by updating your profile preferences or contacting the facility.

To exercise any of these rights, you may update your profile directly in the client portal, contact your pet care facility, or email us at hello@spendbydesign.co.

8. Children's Privacy

This Service is intended for use by adults (18 years of age or older) booking pet care services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our portal, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

9. Sign-In & Session Information

Access to the client portal requires signing in with your Google account. We use Google's secure sign-in service only to verify your identity — we do not access your Gmail, Google Drive, Google Calendar, or any other Google service on your behalf. Your session is remembered for convenience in your browser and is cleared when you sign out.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you through the portal. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
